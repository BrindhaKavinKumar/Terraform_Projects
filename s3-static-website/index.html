Kanchivaram Sarees

Kanchipuram (Kanchi) is a historic temple city in Tamil Nadu. Its weaving legacy is famous for sarees with a rich silk body, striking borders, and a grand pallu — often inspired by temple art.

A short history

Kanchipuram silks are celebrated for their craftsmanship and longevity. Traditional design language draws from the visual rhythm of temples — gopuram-like borders, geometric checks, florals, and auspicious motifs. Over generations, weavers refined these elements into the iconic “Kanchivaram look” loved for weddings and festivals.

Weavers & Craft

Every Kanchivaram saree begins on the loom — where skilled hands translate tradition into thread. The beauty is not only in how it looks, but in how it holds its form, its shine, and its story over time.

RIND celebrates weaving as living heritage — a craft carried forward through families, workshops, and communities that honor detail and patience.
